Que. What is List? How will you reverse a list?


*List is a group of diffrent type of data

-User can reverse the list by giving jumping in negetive or using a string reverse function.

Ex.1:
l = [1,2,1.1,2.2,"tops",True,"Python",10,20,1,2]
